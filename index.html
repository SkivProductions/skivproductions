For Schijt Situatie, I developed and produced the "Where will you be when nature strikes?" campaign, a playful, story-driven concept emphasizing the brand's ability to deliver eco-friendly toilet paper anywhere in Belgium. The campaign included multiple video pieces, such as the main ad "Where will you be when nature calls" and a complementary TV sketch, both designed to blend humor with brand awareness.
